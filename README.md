## About

I work at the intersection of **machine learning** and **systems engineering**. My day-to-day is split between:

- **Training** time-series and LLM models (clean data flows, reproducible experiments, evaluation).
- **Inference/Serving** with FastAPI/WebSocket services and pragmatic response validation/A-B testing.
- **AI infrastructure**: provisioning **GPU hosts**, Docker/Compose builds, registries, hardening, and **observability** (logs/metrics) on Linux.

On the product side, I’m comfortable bridging **ML backends** and **frontends**:
- **3D HMI components** (Maschinenfabrik Reinhausen), integration of inference services into UI modules.
- **Monitoring & reliability** on **HPC clusters** (Brunel/Airbus): Splunk dashboards, health/status tooling for jobs and nodes.
- As **CTO & co-founder** (NexPatch/FinPatch), I coordinate **API design**, **UI/UX**, and model lifecycle (train → serve → observe).

### Current challenges I’m tackling
- **Reliable long-running training** on shared GPU servers (restarts, checkpoints, and drift-aware evaluation).
- **Deterministic inference** under load: batching, latency/throughput trade-offs, and safe fallbacks.
- **Reproducibility & MLOps hygiene**: versioned data/model artifacts, experiment tracking, and clean promotion to prod.
- **Security & hardening** for deployed services (secrets, minimal images, update policies).
- **Developer experience**: fast local repro of prod issues with containerized stacks.
